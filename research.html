Research & Notes

My work explores the rich interplay between differential geometry, complex geometry, and the theory of Higgs bundles — studying moduli spaces and flat connections through geometric and analytic tools.

01

Research Interests

Differential Geometry → Complex Geometry → Higgs Bundles
Core

Differential Geometry

Smooth manifolds and their geometric structures — connections, curvature, and the interplay between local and global properties of geometric spaces.

Core

Complex Geometry

Geometric structures on complex manifolds, holomorphic vector bundles, and analytic tools arising from complex analysis and differential geometry.

Focus

Higgs Bundles

Pairs of a holomorphic vector bundle and a Higgs field, connected to flat connections, surface group representations, and non-abelian Hodge theory.

Related

Teichmüller Theory

Moduli of Riemann surfaces and hyperbolic structures, and their connections to representation varieties and higher Teichmüller spaces.
